Questions

Find answers to common questions about creating and managing time capsules.
How to create a time capsule?
To create a time capsule, simply sign up for an account, record your video message, and choose a future date for it to be delivered. You can also add tags and descriptions to make it easier to find later.
How long can I record a video message?
You can record a video message of up to 5 minutes in length. If your message exceeds this limit, you may need to trim it down or consider splitting it into multiple capsules.
Can I edit my time capsule after creating it?
No, once a time capsule is created, it cannot be edited. This ensures the authenticity and integrity of the messages recorded.
How do I view my time capsules?
To view your time capsules, simply go to your account dashboard and click on the 'Time Capsules' tab. There, you will find a list of all your created capsules.
Can I delete a time capsule?
Yes, you can delete a time capsule at any time. However, please note that once deleted, the contents of the capsule will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered.
